ERA 1 — THE GARAGE YEARS  (2014 — 2017)  "VIBEZ"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

NullScale was founded in 2014 in Chad Vibington III's parents' garage, a garage
that did not exist, in a city that has since been renamed.

The original product, "Vibez", was a mobile application that did the following:

  - Detected the user's mood via the front-facing camera.
  - Sent the mood to a server.
  - Did nothing with the mood.
  - Charged $1.99/mo.

Vibez had 14 paying users. Three of them were Chad's parents. Two of them
were Chad. The remaining nine have asked not to be named, and have continued
to be billed.

KEY EVENTS:

  2014-04-12    Vibez incorporated as a Delaware C-Corp on a napkin.
                The napkin was patented.

  2014-06-01    First "all-hands meeting." Attendance: 1. Duration: 11 hours.

  2015-02      Vibez raised a $200,000 pre-seed round from a man Chad met
               at a juice bar. The man's name was "Brett." There were many
               Bretts. We are not sure which one.

  2015-09      First customer churns. Vibez introduces "Anti-Churn Protocol
               v0", which consisted of Chad personally calling the customer
               at 3am for six weeks. Customer eventually re-subscribed under
               duress.

  2016-05      Vibez "raises" Series A from a fictional VC named "Synergy
               Partners," which turns out to be Chad in a wig. The round was
               unwound. The wig was kept on the cap table.

  2017-01      Vibez pivots from "mood detection" to "mood enforcement."
                The app is rebranded to "VIBEZ Pro."

ERA 2 — THE PIVOT YEARS  (2017 — 2020)  "VIBEOS"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In 2017, Chad pivoted Vibez from a B2C mood-enforcement app to a B2B
"vibe-native operating system." The new product was named VibeOS.

VibeOS was, technically, a Chrome extension. It rendered a fake desktop on
top of the user's existing desktop. It could not be uninstalled.

This era saw the introduction of several foundational concepts that remain
core to the NullScale product line today:

  - The "Vibe Dashboard" (a window with charts that did not chart anything).
  - The "Daemon" (a background process that pinged the user's database
    10,000 times per second on the free tier).
  - "Friction-as-a-Service" (FaaS): the company's first sustainable revenue
    model, where users paid the company to NOT contact their database.
  - The unclosable alert (introduced in v1.4, never removed).

KEY EVENTS:

  2017-08      VibeOS Series B led by an LP named "Greg." Greg later turned
                out to also be Chad. The wig from 2016 was reused. Greg is
                still on the cap table.

  2018-02-29   "Leap Day Layoffs": Chad lays off 100% of staff (including
                himself) and immediately rehires himself with a 14% raise
                and additional equity.

  2018-Q4      VibeOS "achieves profitability" by reclassifying customer
                lawsuits as "engagement metrics."

  2019-03      The first BSOD ascension event. A customer using VibeOS
                Premium reports that their browser tab "achieved transcendence"
                and could no longer be closed without rebooting reality.
                Chad calls this a "feature." It is, formally, now a feature.

  2019-10      VibeOS Inc. is featured in The VibeTimes (a publication owned
                by VibeOS Inc.) as "the most disruptive company in any market
                that VibeOS Inc. owns publications about."

  2020-03      VibeOS's first publicly-disclosed data breach. 14 trillion rows
                of fictional data are leaked. None of it is real, but all of
                it is now liability. Chad declares this "synergy."

ERA 3 — THE NAMING CRISIS  (2020 — 2024)  "SYNERGIST.IO"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In 2020, VibeOS Inc. discovered, mid-IPO roadshow, that "VibeOS" was the
name of an actual existing company headquartered in Berlin. The actual
existing company sent a polite cease-and-desist. Chad replied, in full:

       "Lol pivoting"

Within 47 minutes, VibeOS was renamed to "Synergist.io". All branding,
documentation, contracts, tattoos, and customer DNA records were updated
overnight. Customers were not notified. Customers continued to receive
invoices addressed to "VibeOS Premium Tier 4 (Synergist.io Edition Pro)."

KEY EVENTS:

  2020-04      Synergist.io officially launches via Slack DM to existing
                customers. Slack DM included a 47-page EULA in plaintext.

  2020-09      Synergist.io introduces the "Vibe Quotient" (VQ): a number
                between 0 and 1.4 that measures a user's spiritual and
                financial alignment with Synergist.io. Customers below
                a VQ of 0.42 are rate-limited. Customers above 1.4 are
                automatically promoted to Vice President.

  2021-Q2     The "Reality Coherence Module" ships in production.
               It does not work. This is also a feature.

  2021-11     Series C-but-actually-D. Lead investor: a sovereign wealth
               fund of an unnamed country that does not exist on most maps.
               Funds are wired in unmarked vibes.

  2022-01     Synergist.io spins out 14 subsidiaries, all called
               Synergist.io. They sue each other. They all win. They all
               settle for stock in each other.

  2022-Q3     Chad gives a TED talk titled "Why Your Customers Owe You
               Their Souls (And How To Collect)." The talk is removed from

  2023-04     The Daemons Interlink is launched. AI agents begin

ERA 4 — THE NAMING CRISIS, AGAIN  (2024 — 2026)  "NULLSCALE"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In late 2024, Synergist.io discovered, mid-acquisition negotiation, that
"Synergist.io" was the name of yet another existing company, this one
headquartered in Toronto and operated by a single retired dental hygienist.
The dental hygienist was sent a brief, courteous email. She replied in full:

       "Are you the company that's been mailing my mother NFTs?"

Within 14 minutes, Synergist.io was renamed to "NullScale."

The new name was selected via a process the marketing team described as
"vibing." Specifically: an LLM was asked to "produce a name that sounds
both terrifying and venture-backed, but means literally nothing." The LLM
produced 4,201 candidates. NullScale ranked 4,200th. It was selected
because it was "humble."

KEY EVENTS:

  2024-Q4     NullScale officially launches. Customers are told via push
              notification at 4:14am local time. The push notification
              includes new EULA terms in 8pt Wingdings.

  2025-Q1     NullScale acquires the concept of Tuesdays in a stock-and-
              vibes deal. Tuesday is now a wholly-owned subsidiary.

  2025-Q2     Chad publicly retires for the first time. Chad publicly
              un-retires 4 hours later, citing "boredom and shareholder
              concern." Chad's chair was kept warm by an intern named Brett.

  2025-Q3     Launch of "GPT-9000," the company's proprietary AI agent.
              GPT-9000 is, internally, just the public ChatGPT API with

              "the most advanced AGI ever deployed in production."

  2025-Q4     NullScale's Anti-Churn Protocol v3 is finalized. It is
