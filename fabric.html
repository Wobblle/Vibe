this is the documents archive for The Fabric.

artifacts are released ~72h after each episode upload. each artifact is a plain-text file. it can be opened in any browser, copied, mirrored, hosted elsewhere. attribution optional. the territory is unchanged. the artifacts are how you mark it.

episodes ship in version-controlled cuts. ep 2.0 is the first canonical cut of episode 2. ep 2.1 will live alongside it, not replace it. the series is a discovery process and formatted for version control.
